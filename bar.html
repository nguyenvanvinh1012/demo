DU BAR | TASTE OF SCENT

Du Bar is an independent project, rooted in the desire to offer a multi-sensory artistic experience of scent at Scentury Concept. Where we found the final piece of that experience was taste, which the sense of smell plays the most important role in shaping.
 
Some people see Du Bar as a place to stop on the way home, others see Du Bar as a destination for escapes from reality, or sometimes part of an indefinable concept of 'home'. town. Du Bar - quiet and peaceful, separating yourself from the daily chaos. Wrapped up in the yeast, only me with myself remained to contemplate a few idle things at the end of the day.

 

 

Each type of wine born has its own aroma. That is why there is a meeting of the roots and the companion named perfume. Each oscillation brings with it the meticulousness of flavor and taste from the bartender. Where we can taste our favorite scent, not just feel it in the air anymore.
 
At Du Bar, we redraw the scent of perfume through the contours of the wine, rewriting the story of the original work with the sound of emotions. The layers of flavors - complex flavors are meticulously crafted, so that anyone can enjoy their own niche cocktail.

 

Du Bar's space is only suitable for wandering souls amidst the bustle of the city
 
Du Bar - Perfume Cocktail Bar, the sophistication, meticulous focus on traditional Classic dishes, with a 'niche' emphasis hidden behind each Signature Cocktail and countless emotions and stories from the works of perfumery. art. Du Bar creates their own niche Cocktails. The hand-crafted liqueur cups for each guest by bartenders are definitely unlike anyone else's in life.

 

Little Song Cocktail - inspired by the work of meow fusciuni with the taste of loneliness

 

Du Bar's emotional vibrations are directed towards the "me", where tired souls find a calm state of mind. The clear scent blocks the memory as it explores deeply into the mind. Then to say, the yeast here is not only the strong alcohol taste but also the soul escaping from reality, suspended in the atmosphere.
 
Get ready for a trip full of artistic nuances with Du Bar.
