THE STORY OF Scentury Concept

Founded in 2017, Scentury Concept was born with the purpose of bringing artistic inspiration hidden behind the material values of niche perfume concept, when those scents are usually only understood as high-class perfumes and are more expensive than other commercial perfumes.

This is the place where we poured our heart and soul to creating the most complete experience of art, focusing on scent and around the harmonious and diverse combination of art forms from all sensory elements: Sound - Image - Taste - Emotion

At Scentury Concept, we offer a unique, personalized scent experience for each customer with the inherent artistic essence of scent. With a different experience style compared to other perfume shops, closest to artistic sensibility, beyond the usual standards. Where not only the sense of smell is used, but also the sight, hearing and touch are awakened by a space completely separate from the noisy, hurried world out there. Scent will always evoke in the subconscious many emotions and associated with the deepest memory, and we also want to be the most beautiful part of your subconscious.
 
Like any other art business, what Scentury Concept sells is experiencing the beauty of art. Scentury Concept is a concept that exists in every customer who comes here, we are not simply a perfume shop just to smell good, or to be classy but where you can directly "touch" the art of scent.

 

Scent is an art form that you can wear on your body, then it becomes your own art, as a 'concept' that is always imprinted in our mind.
